👋 Behind BookTogether

About Us

We are a team of three engineering students and graduates who share a passion for traveling, meeting new people, and building useful digital tools. BookTogether started from a simple observation: many travelers go to the same destinations at the same time, yet most people still book and pay for accommodation alone. We believe there should be a smarter and more social way to travel.

Our goal is to make travel more affordable by helping people find others heading to the same place so they can share accommodation costs and plan their trips together. We're currently building the first version of BookTogether and are excited to learn from travelers who want to explore the world while spending less.
